About Indy's Indoor Bark Park

Indy's Indoor Bark Park was created by dog lovers who saw a simple need: Indianapolis dogs deserve a safe, fun place to play no matter the weather. Indiana's unpredictable seasons — scorching summers, icy winters, and everything in between — mean outdoor dog parks often sit empty for months at a time. Our indoor facility changes that equation entirely.

Our 15,000 square foot facility was designed from the ground up for canine comfort and safety. Rubberized sport flooring protects joints and provides traction. Industrial ventilation systems keep the air fresh. Professional-grade cleaning protocols maintain a healthy, odor-free environment. Every detail was considered with your dog's wellbeing in mind.

We believe that regular socialization and exercise are essential to a dog's physical and mental health. Dogs who play regularly are happier, better behaved, and healthier than those who don't get enough stimulation. Our park provides a structured environment where dogs can be dogs — running, playing, and socializing under professional supervision.
