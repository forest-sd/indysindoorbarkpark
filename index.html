Year-Round Fun for Your Pup

Welcome to Indy's Indoor Bark Park, where the fun never stops regardless of the weather. Our climate-controlled indoor facility provides a safe, clean, and stimulating environment for dogs to play, socialize, and exercise year-round in the heart of Indianapolis.

Whether it's a sweltering summer day, a freezing winter morning, or a rainy afternoon, your furry friend deserves quality playtime. Our spacious facility features separate areas for large and small dogs, agility equipment, splash zones, and plenty of room for your pup to run, wrestle, and make new friends.

Off-Leash Play

Spacious indoor areas with rubberized flooring, tunnels, ramps, and obstacles. Separate zones for large and small breeds ensure every dog plays safely and comfortably.

Supervised Fun

Trained staff monitor all play sessions to ensure safety and positive interactions. We maintain strict vaccination and temperament requirements for all visiting dogs.

Special Events

Birthday parties, puppy socials, breed meetups, and holiday events. From "Yappy Hours" to agility clinics, there's always something exciting happening at the Bark Park.
