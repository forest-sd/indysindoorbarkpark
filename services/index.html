Services & Memberships

Day Passes

Drop in anytime during operating hours. Includes full access to play areas, water stations, and all park amenities. First visit is always half price!

Monthly Membership

Unlimited visits for one flat monthly rate. Members also get discounts on events, grooming, and retail. Multi-dog discounts available.

Doggy Daycare

Full-day supervised care with structured play sessions, rest periods, and individual attention. Perfect for working pet parents who want their dog active and happy.

Birthday Parties

Celebrate your pup's special day with a private party area, dog-safe cake, party favors, and dedicated play time with their furry friends.

Grooming

Self-serve wash stations and professional grooming services. Get your pup cleaned up after a big play session before heading home.

Training Classes

Basic obedience, puppy kindergarten, agility fundamentals, and socialization classes led by certified trainers in our dedicated training space.
